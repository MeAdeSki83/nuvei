NUVEI “$100 Amazon gift card” CONTEST (the “Contest”)

 

 

This Contest is only open to residents of Canada, excluding the province of Quebec, and is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the laws of Canada applicable therein. This promotion is in no way sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with, Survey Monkey. You are providing your information to Nuvei Technologies Corp. and not to Survey Monkey.

 

  1. SPONSOR. The sponsor of this Contest is Nuvei Technologies Corp. (the “Sponsor” or

“Nuvei”), having offices at 1100 Rene Levesque, Suite 900, Montreal, Quebec, H3B 4N4.

  1. CONTEST PERIODS. The Contest period shall commence on January 1, 2021, at 12:00:01 p.m. EST and will run until March 31, 2021 at 11:59:59 p.m. EST (“Contest Period”).
  1. ELIGIBILITY. This Contest is open to all individuals who are clients of the Sponsor, are currently provided with payment processing services by the Sponsor, are Canadian residents, excluding the province of Quebec, and have reached the age of majority in the province or territory in which they reside. The following persons shall not be eligible: employees, representatives and agents of the Sponsor, or their respective affiliates and, if applicable, their respective advertising or promotion agencies, contest judges, contest prize suppliers, and any other companies engaged in the development, production, or distribution of materials for this Contest, and members of the immediate families of, or persons domiciled with, any of the above.
  1. HOW TO ENTER AND NO PURCHASE NECESSARY.
    • 1) You will automatically be entered into the Contest if you, within the Contest Period, fill out the Nuvei customer satisfaction survey sent to you via email on behalf of the Sponsor. No purchase is required by entrant to enter into the Contest.

2) To be entered into the Contest, you must complete and send back the survey to the Sponsor before March 31, 2021. The Sponsor will not be responsible for lost, misdirected, stolen, illegible or late requests.

3) Free alternate method of entry: you may also enter into the Contest by sending an email to: support@merchant-support.com, explaining, in about two hundred (200) words, before March 31, 2021, how use of this $100CAD Amazon gift card will help your business grow.

  1. NUMBER OF ENTRIES. Limit of one (1) entry into the Contest per entrant.
  1. PRIZES. There are five (5) prizes available to be won. Each prize is a $100CAD Amazon gift card. The value of the prize is $100CAD. The Sponsor reserves the right, at its sole and absolute discretion, to substitute the prize for prize of approximately equal or greater retail value, at any time and for any reason, including, without limitation, by way of cash award. Cash value is $100 CAD. The odds of winning the prize depends on the number of eligible surveys received.
  1. DRAW. The random draw of five (5) Winners will take place at the Sponsor’s offices located at 1100 Boulevard Rene-Levesque West, Suite 900, Montreal, Quebec, H3B 4N4 on April 5, 2021 at 12:00:01 p.m. EST from among all eligible entries received during the Contest Period. Prize must be accepted as awarded and cannot be substituted, transferred, exchanged or surrendered for cash.
  1. HOW TO WIN AND CLAIM A PRIZE. Any selected entrant will be notified within approximately seven (7) days from the draw date by phone or email and instructed on how to claim his/her prize. To be declared a Winner, each selected entrant must answer the time-limited survey questions (or alternatively, enter the contest in accordance with Section 4, paragraph 3), without assistance of any kind, whether mechanical or otherwise, to be administered by email, must sign a form of Declaration of Compliance and Release (see Section 9 below) and otherwise comply with these official contest rules. Awarding of a prize is subject to verification and compliance with these official contest rules. Entrants who have not complied with these official contest rules will be disqualified. If a selected entrant cannot be located or does not respond or comply with all of these official contest rules within fourteen (14) days of notification, the entrant will be disqualified and forfeits the prize and the Sponsor may, at its sole and absolute discretion, select by random draw another eligible entrant whom the Sponsor or its representatives will attempt to contact, and who will be subject to disqualification, in the same manner. Allow up to six (6) weeks from the date of the draw for verification of contest Winners and prize fulfillment. The Winners will be contacted following verification with details on how to redeem the prize. Sponsor will not be responsible for failed attempts to notify selected entrants.

Winner is solely responsible for reporting and paying any and all applicable taxes related to the prize. Winner must provide Sponsor with valid identification and a valid taxpayer identification number, social insurance number or social security number, before any prize will be awarded.
